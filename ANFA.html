Who We Are

A Sanctuary for
Nomadic Heritage

The Arab Nomadic Friendly Association (ANFA) was established to honor the resilience and hospitality of the Bedouin tribes. In a rapidly modernizing world, the silence of the desert and the wisdom of its people are treasures at risk of being lost.

Headquartered in the heart of the Middle East, we operate as a cultural nexus, supporting sustainable development for nomadic communities while sharing their profound artistry and philosophy with the international community.
